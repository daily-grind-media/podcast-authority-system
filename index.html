Podcast Discovery System
Collect strategic information before recording — never go in blind
Core
Objective

Collect strategic information from the client before a single camera turns on. Understand their offer, audience, pain points, and goals.

Why It Matters

A discovery form separates DGM from amateur podcast producers. It makes the session strategic, not random. It makes editing easier and content more targeted.

Who Owns This

Lo sends and reviews all discovery forms. Claude analyzes responses. Output is stored in Notion + Google Drive.

Current Tools
Vercel + Formspree
Public-facing form. Simple, fast, no-login required for clients.
Notion
Store discovery responses. Link to client and project records.
Claude
Analyze responses. Generate strategy, content pillars, lead magnet ideas.
Google Drive + Dropbox
Store raw discovery PDFs and analysis outputs per client.
When & How to Send Discovery
Timing Protocol
  • Send discovery form immediately after the intro call — before scheduling the shoot
  • Set a 48-hour deadline for completion
  • Follow up personally if not received within 24 hours
  • Never schedule the session without a completed discovery form

Podcast Structure Creation
9-part framework engineered to generate maximum content
Core

The podcast structure is not written to be a great interview. It is engineered to be a content extraction machine. Every segment is designed to produce hooks, clips, reels, stories, and authority moments.

9-Part Interview Framework
#SegmentCore QuestionContent Opportunities
1Who You Help"Walk me through exactly who you work with and what they're struggling with when they first come to you."Niche reel, audience-recognition hook, testimonial setup
2Origin Story"Take me back to the beginning — what happened in your life that made this work feel necessary?"Personal story reel, authority establishment, emotional content
3Biggest Client Problems"What are the 3 most common mistakes people make before they find you?"Myth-busting reels, educational clips, objection content
4Coaching Philosophy"What do you believe about [topic] that most coaches in your space have completely wrong?"Contrarian reel, thought leadership, hot take content
5Client Success Story"Tell me about a specific client — where were they when they started, what did you do together, and where are they now?"Testimonial reel, case study, social proof content
6Myths & Mistakes"What's the biggest lie people in your industry are telling clients that actually holds them back?"Hot take reel, educational blog, viral hook content
7Practical Advice"If someone could only do one thing this week to [get result], what would you tell them?"Quick-tip reel, short-form clip, newsletter content
8Offer Positioning"For someone sitting at home right now who is [problem] — what does working with you actually look like?"Offer awareness reel, sales page copy, CRM email
9Closing Lesson"What's the one thing you wish everyone in your audience understood that would change everything for them?"Inspirational reel, newsletter closer, social quote
